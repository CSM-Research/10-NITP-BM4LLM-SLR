- Selection Criteria:
--------------------------

INCLUSION CRITERIA:

(IC1) Article is in the Computer Science/Software Engineering Area; 
(IC2) Article discusses the use of LLMs in studies selected in an SLR. 


EXCLUSION CRITERIA 

(EC1) results originated from the domain of medicine, 
(EC2) not about the application of LLMs for SLRs, 
(EC3) duplicate, 
(EC4) not written in English, 
(EC5) not a primary study or not peer-reviewed material. The selection process applying these criteria resulted in 13 relevant studies


--------------------------
- Extraction form
--------------------------

Q1 - Which are the biases caused by usage of LLMs for SLRs?; 
Q2 - Which factors (external factors, contextual constraints, or environmental considerations) impact the usage of LLMs in systematic reviews?
